NEON

Neon is a noble gas, known for its inertness and bright glow when used in neon signs.
It is a colorless, odorless gas at room temperature and is found in trace amounts in the Earth's atmosphere.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Neon is chemically inert and does not form compounds under normal conditions.
💣
Physical Properties
Neon has a boiling point of -246.05°C and a melting point of -248.59°C.
🎯
Uses
Neon is used in neon signs, high-voltage indicators, and as a coolant in some types of lasers.
