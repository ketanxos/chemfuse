ARSENIC

Arsenic is a metalloid known for its toxicity.
It has been used historically as a poison and in pesticides.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Arsenic forms compounds with metals and non-metals. It is highly toxic in its elemental form and in many of its compounds.
💣
Physical Properties
Arsenic has a boiling point of 614°C and a melting point of 817°C.
🎯
Uses
Arsenic was historically used as a poison and in pesticides. It is also used in certain alloys and semiconductors.
