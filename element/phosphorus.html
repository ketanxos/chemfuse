PHOSPHORUS

Phosphorus is a vital element for life, found in DNA, RNA, and ATP.
It is commonly used in fertilizers, detergents, and as a component in various industrial processes.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Phosphorus reacts with oxygen to form phosphorus oxides. It also forms compounds with most elements, including hydrogen and chlorine.
💣
Physical Properties
Phosphorus has a boiling point of 280°C and a melting point of 44.2°C.
🎯
Uses
Phosphorus is used in fertilizers, fireworks, and in the production of phosphoric acid.
