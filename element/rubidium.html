RUBIDIUM

Rubidium is an alkali metal known for its high reactivity.
It is used in atomic clocks and research applications.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Rubidium reacts explosively with water and is used in research due to its highly reactive nature.
💣
Physical Properties
Rubidium has a boiling point of 688°C and a melting point of 39.3°C.
🎯
Uses
Rubidium is used in atomic clocks, research, and in some special glasses and ceramics.
