BORON

Boron is a metalloid element that is essential for the growth of plants. It is used in glass, ceramics, and detergents.
It is a poor conductor of electricity and has a high melting point.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Boron forms covalent compounds and reacts with acids. It is a strong oxidizing agent.
💣
Physical Properties
Boron has a boiling point of 4000°C and a melting point of 2075°C.
🎯
Uses
Boron is used in the manufacture of borosilicate glass, ceramics, and as a fire retardant.
