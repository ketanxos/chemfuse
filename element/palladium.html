PALLADIUM

Palladium is a precious metal used in catalytic converters and in various high-end electronics.
It is also used in jewelry and watchmaking.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Palladium is highly resistant to oxidation and is used in catalytic converters to reduce emissions.
💣
Physical Properties
Palladium has a boiling point of 2963°C and a melting point of 1554°C.
🎯
Uses
Palladium is used in catalytic converters, jewelry, and electronics.
