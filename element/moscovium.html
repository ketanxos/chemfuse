MOSCOVIUM

Moscovium is a synthetic element with the symbol Mc and atomic number 115.
It is an extremely radioactive element that was first synthesized in 2003.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Due to its short half-life, the chemical properties of Moscovium are not well-studied. It is predicted to behave similarly to bismuth and antimony.
💣
Physical Properties
Moscovium is a heavy metal and is expected to be a solid at room temperature. It has a very short half-life, making it difficult to study its physical properties.
🎯
Uses
Moscovium has no known practical uses due to its extreme radioactivity and short half-life. It is primarily of interest for scientific research.
