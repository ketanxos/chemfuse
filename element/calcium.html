CALCIUM

Calcium is a soft, gray alkaline earth metal.
It is essential for living organisms and is used in construction materials such as cement and lime.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Calcium reacts with water to form calcium hydroxide and hydrogen gas. It also reacts with oxygen to form calcium oxide.
💣
Physical Properties
Calcium has a boiling point of 1484°C and a melting point of 842°C.
🎯
Uses
Calcium is used in cement, lime, and as a dietary supplement in the form of calcium carbonate.
