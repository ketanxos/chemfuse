HYDROGEN

Hydrogen is the lightest and most abundant element in the universe.
It is a colorless, odorless, and highly flammable gas at room temperature.
Hydrogen plays a critical role in various industrial applications, including the production of ammonia and as a clean fuel source in fuel cells.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Hydrogen is highly reactive and forms compounds with most elements, such as water (H2O) and methane (CH4).
💣
Physical Properties
Hydrogen is a colorless, odorless gas with a boiling point of -252.87°C and a melting point of -259.16°C.
🎯
Uses
Hydrogen is used in fuel cells, as a rocket propellant, and in the production of ammonia for fertilizers.
