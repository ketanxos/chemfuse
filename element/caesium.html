CAESIUM

Cesium is an alkali metal used in atomic clocks and as a catalyst in various chemical reactions.
It is highly reactive and used in specialized applications.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Cesium is highly reactive and forms compounds such as cesium chloride (CsCl) and cesium carbonate (Cs2CO3).
💣
Physical Properties
Cesium has a boiling point of 671°C and a melting point of 28.5°C.
🎯
Uses
Cesium is used in atomic clocks, in oil drilling, and as a catalyst in various reactions.
