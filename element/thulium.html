THULIUM

Thulium is a lanthanide used in medical lasers and high-performance materials.
It is also used in various optical devices and as a dopant in certain types of lasers.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Thulium forms compounds such as thulium oxide (Tm2O3) and thulium chloride (TmCl3).
💣
Physical Properties
Thulium has a boiling point of 5458°C and a melting point of 1545°C.
🎯
Uses
Thulium is used in medical lasers, optical devices, and high-performance materials.
