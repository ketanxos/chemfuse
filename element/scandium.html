SCANDIUM

Scandium is a soft, silvery-white metal.
It is used in alloys and has applications in aerospace and sports equipment.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Scandium reacts with oxygen and forms scandium oxide. It also forms alloys with aluminum and other metals.
💣
Physical Properties
Scandium has a boiling point of 2836°C and a melting point of 1541°C.
🎯
Uses
Scandium is used in aerospace components, sports equipment, and in certain metal alloys.
