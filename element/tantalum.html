TANTALUM

Tantalum is a transition metal used in electronic components and high-temperature alloys.
It is highly resistant to corrosion and is used in various medical and aerospace applications.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Tantalum forms compounds such as tantalum pentoxide (Ta2O5) and tantalum chloride (TaCl5).
💣
Physical Properties
Tantalum has a boiling point of 5458°C and a melting point of 3017°C.
🎯
Uses
Tantalum is used in electronic components, high-temperature alloys, and medical applications.
