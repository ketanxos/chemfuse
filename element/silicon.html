SILICON

Silicon is a metalloid with a key role in electronics and computer technology.
It is a hard, brittle crystalline solid with a blue-grey metallic luster.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Silicon reacts with oxygen to form silicon dioxide. It also forms compounds with many metals.
💣
Physical Properties
Silicon has a boiling point of 2900°C and a melting point of 1414°C.
🎯
Uses
Silicon is used in semiconductors, solar panels, and as a material in glass and ceramics.
