KRYPTON

Krypton is a noble gas that is used in lighting and in certain types of lasers.
It is a colorless, odorless gas.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Krypton is chemically inert and does not readily form compounds. It is used in high-performance lighting.
💣
Physical Properties
Krypton has a boiling point of -153.4°C and a melting point of -157.4°C.
🎯
Uses
Krypton is used in high-intensity lamps, laser systems, and for various scientific applications.
