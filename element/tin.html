TIN

Tin is a post-transition metal used in various alloys and coatings.
It is known for its corrosion resistance and use in soldering.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Tin forms various compounds such as tin chloride (SnCl2) and tin oxide (SnO).
💣
Physical Properties
Tin has a boiling point of 2602°C and a melting point of 231.93°C.
🎯
Uses
Tin is used in soldering, plating, and as an alloying agent in bronze.
