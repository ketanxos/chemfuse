NICKEL

Nickel is a silvery-white metal known for its resistance to corrosion.
It is widely used in alloys such as stainless steel and in batteries./div>
🧪
Chemical Properties
Nickel is resistant to oxidation and corrosion. It is used in many alloys, including stainless steel.
💣
Physical Properties
Nickel has a boiling point of 2913°C and a melting point of 1455°C.
🎯
Uses
Nickel is used in stainless steel, coins, and rechargeable batteries.
