NIOBUM

Niobium is a transition metal used in superconductors and various high-strength alloys.
It is known for its resistance to corrosion.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Niobium forms a stable oxide layer, making it resistant to corrosion and oxidation.
💣
Physical Properties
Niobium has a boiling point of 4744°C and a melting point of 2477°C.
🎯
Uses
Niobium is used in superconductors, jewelry, and high-strength alloys.
