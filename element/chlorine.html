CHLORINE

Chlorine is a yellow-green gas with a strong, distinctive smell.
It is widely used in disinfectants and bleach, and is an important element in the production of various chemicals.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Chlorine reacts with metals to form chlorides and with hydrogen to form hydrogen chloride.
💣
Physical Properties
Chlorine has a boiling point of -34.04°C and a melting point of -101.5°C.
🎯
Uses
Chlorine is used in water purification, bleach, and as a precursor to various industrial chemicals.
