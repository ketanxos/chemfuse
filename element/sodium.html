SODIUM

Sodium is a highly reactive metal known for its role in salt formation and its importance in biological systems.
It is a soft, silvery metal and reacts vigorously with water.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Sodium reacts violently with water to form sodium hydroxide and hydrogen gas. It also forms compounds with halogens.
💣
Physical Properties
Sodium has a boiling point of 883°C and a melting point of 97.72°C.
🎯
Uses
Sodium is used in table salt, sodium vapor lamps, and as a reagent in chemical syntheses.
