CHROMIUM

Chromium is a shiny, hard metal with a high melting point.
It is used in stainless steel, plating, and pigments.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Chromium reacts with oxygen to form chromium oxides. It is also used in various alloys and catalysts.
💣
Physical Properties
Chromium has a boiling point of 2671°C and a melting point of 1907°C.
🎯
Uses
Chromium is used in stainless steel, chrome plating, and in producing pigments.
