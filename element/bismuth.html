BISMUTH

Bismuth is a brittle metal with a pinkish hue.
It is used in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and as an alloy in various applications.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Bismuth forms compounds such as bismuth oxide (Bi2O3) and bismuth sulfide (Bi2S3).
💣
Physical Properties
Bismuth has a boiling point of 1564°C and a melting point of 271.3°C.
🎯
Uses
Bismuth is used in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and as an alloy in various applications.
