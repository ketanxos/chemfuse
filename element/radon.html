RADON

Radon is a colorless, odorless, radioactive gas.
It is produced naturally from the decay of uranium and thorium and poses health risks due to its radioactivity.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Radon forms compounds such as radon fluoride (RnF) and radon chloride (RnCl2).
💣
Physical Properties
Radon has a boiling point of -61.8°C and a melting point of -71°C.
🎯
Uses
Radon is used in cancer treatment and has limited industrial applications.
