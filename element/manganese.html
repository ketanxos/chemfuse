MANGANESE

Manganese is a brittle, silvery-white metal.
It is used in steel production and as an alloying agent.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Manganese reacts with oxygen to form manganese oxides. It is also used as a catalyst in various chemical reactions.
💣
Physical Properties
Manganese has a boiling point of 2095°C and a melting point of 1244°C.
🎯
Uses
Manganese is used in steel production, batteries, and as a pigment in ceramics and glass.
