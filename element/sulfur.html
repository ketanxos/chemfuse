SULFUR

Sulfur is a non-metal element with a distinct yellow color.
It is commonly used in the production of sulfuric acid and in various industrial processes.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Sulfur reacts with oxygen to form sulfur dioxide and sulfur trioxide. It also reacts with metals to form sulfides.
💣
Physical Properties
Sulfur has a boiling point of 444.6°C and a melting point of 115.2°C.
🎯
Uses
Sulfur is used in the production of sulfuric acid, fertilizers, and in vulcanization of rubber.
