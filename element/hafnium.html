HAFNIUM

Hafnium is a transition metal used in various high-temperature applications and nuclear reactors.
It is also used in the production of certain types of alloys and ceramics.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Hafnium forms compounds such as hafnium oxide (HfO2) and hafnium chloride (HfCl4).
💣
Physical Properties
Hafnium has a boiling point of 4876°C and a melting point of 2233°C.
🎯
Uses
Hafnium is used in high-temperature applications, nuclear reactors, and ceramics.
