RADIUM

Radium is a highly radioactive metal.
It was historically used in luminous paint and has applications in cancer treatment.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Radium forms compounds such as radium chloride (RaCl2) and radium sulfate (RaSO4).
💣
Physical Properties
Radium has a boiling point of 1413°C and a melting point of 700°C.
🎯
Uses
Radium is used in cancer treatment and was historically used in luminous paint.
