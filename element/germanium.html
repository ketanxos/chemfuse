GERMANIUM

Germanium is a brittle, silvery-white metalloid.
It is used in semiconductors and fiber optics.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Germanium is chemically similar to silicon and is used in various electronic components.
💣
Physical Properties
Germanium has a boiling point of 2833°C and a melting point of 938.25°C.
🎯
Uses
Germanium is used in fiber optics, semiconductors, and infrared optics.
