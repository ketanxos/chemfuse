MAGNESIUM

Magnesium is a lightweight metal known for its strength and applications in various alloys.
It is essential for biological functions and is commonly used in construction materials.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Magnesium reacts with water to form magnesium hydroxide and hydrogen gas. It also forms compounds with most elements.
💣
Physical Properties
Magnesium has a boiling point of 1090°C and a melting point of 650°C.
🎯
Uses
Magnesium is used in alloys, fireworks, and as a dietary supplement.
