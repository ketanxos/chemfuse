DYSPROSIUM

Dysprosium is a lanthanide used in high-performance magnets and nuclear reactors.
It is also employed in various laser applications and as a neutron absorber.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Dysprosium forms compounds such as dysprosium oxide (Dy2O3) and dysprosium chloride (DyCl3).
💣
Physical Properties
Dysprosium has a boiling point of 2840°C and a melting point of 1412°C.
🎯
Uses
Dysprosium is used in high-performance magnets, lasers, and nuclear reactors.
