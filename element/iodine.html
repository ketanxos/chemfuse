IODINE

Iodine is a halogen used in medicine and as a disinfectant.
It is also important in the synthesis of thyroid hormones.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Iodine forms compounds such as iodine monochloride (ICl) and iodine pentafluoride (IF5).
💣
Physical Properties
Iodine has a boiling point of 184.3°C and a melting point of 113.7°C.
🎯
Uses
Iodine is used as a disinfectant, in iodized salt, and in the synthesis of thyroid hormones.
