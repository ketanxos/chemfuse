SILVER

Silver is a precious metal known for its high electrical conductivity and use in jewelry and silverware.
It is also used in various industrial applications.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Silver is highly reactive and forms compounds such as silver chloride (AgCl).
💣
Physical Properties
Silver has a boiling point of 2162°C and a melting point of 961.8°C.
🎯
Uses
Silver is used in jewelry, electronics, and as an antimicrobial agent.
