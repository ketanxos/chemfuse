STRONTIUM

Strontium is an alkali earth metal used in fireworks and in the production of ferrite magnets.
It is similar to calcium in its chemical properties.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Strontium reacts with water and forms a hydroxide. It is used in producing red colors in fireworks.
💣
Physical Properties
Strontium has a boiling point of 1382°C and a melting point of 777°C.
🎯
Uses
Strontium is used in fireworks, magnets, and in some medical treatments.
