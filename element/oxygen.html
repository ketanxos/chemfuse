OXYGEN

Oxygen is a vital element for life on Earth. It is a colorless, odorless gas and the most abundant element in the Earth's crust.
It supports combustion and is essential for respiration in aerobic organisms.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Oxygen supports combustion and reacts with most elements to form oxides.
💣
Physical Properties
Oxygen has a boiling point of -182.96°C and a melting point of -218.79°C.
🎯
Uses
Oxygen is used in medical breathing devices, in water treatment, and as an industrial gas for cutting and welding.
