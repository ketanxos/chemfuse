LANTHANUM

Lanthanum is a lanthanide used in various catalysts and high-performance materials.
It is also used in optical glasses and phosphors.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Lanthanum forms compounds such as lanthanum oxide (La2O3) and lanthanum chloride (LaCl3).
💣
Physical Properties
Lanthanum has a boiling point of 3464°C and a melting point of 1191°C.
🎯
Uses
Lanthanum is used in catalysts, optical glasses, and phosphors.
