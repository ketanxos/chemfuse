FRANCIUM

Francium is a highly radioactive metal.
It is the least stable of the first 101 elements and has no significant commercial applications.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Francium forms compounds such as francium chloride (FrCl) and francium hydroxide (FrOH).
💣
Physical Properties
Francium has a boiling point and melting point that are not well-defined due to its rarity and radioactivity.
🎯
Uses
Francium has no significant commercial applications due to its rarity and radioactivity.
