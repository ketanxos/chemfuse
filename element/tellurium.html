TELLURIUM

Tellurium is a metalloid used in semiconductors and as an additive to improve the properties of other metals.
It is also used in the production of thermoelectric materials.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Tellurium is chemically reactive and forms compounds such as tellurium dioxide (TeO2).
💣
Physical Properties
Tellurium has a boiling point of 450°C and a melting point of 452°C.
🎯
Uses
Tellurium is used in alloys, semiconductors, and as a thermoelectric material.
