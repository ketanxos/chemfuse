NEODYMIUM

Neodymium is a lanthanide used in powerful magnets and laser applications.
It is also used in various optical and electronic devices.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Neodymium forms compounds such as neodymium oxide (Nd2O3) and neodymium chloride (NdCl3).
💣
Physical Properties
Neodymium has a boiling point of 3103°C and a melting point of 1024°C.
🎯
Uses
Neodymium is used in powerful magnets, lasers, and various optical devices.
