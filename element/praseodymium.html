PRASEODYMIUM

Praseodymium is a lanthanide used in high-strength magnets and certain types of glass.
It is also used in various alloy and ceramic applications.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Praseodymium forms compounds such as praseodymium oxide (Pr6O11) and praseodymium chloride (PrCl3).
💣
Physical Properties
Praseodymium has a boiling point of 3290°C and a melting point of 931°C.
🎯
Uses
Praseodymium is used in magnets, glass, and ceramics, and in various alloy applications.
