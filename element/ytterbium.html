YTTERBIUM

Ytterbium is a lanthanide used in various alloys and high-performance materials.
It is also employed in lasers and as a dopant in certain types of glass.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Ytterbium forms compounds such as ytterbium oxide (Yb2O3) and ytterbium chloride (YbCl3).
💣
Physical Properties
Ytterbium has a boiling point of 1196°C and a melting point of 819°C.
🎯
Uses
Ytterbium is used in lasers, alloys, and high-performance materials.
