TUNGSTEN

Tungsten is a transition metal with the highest melting point and boiling point of all elements.
It is used in electrical contacts, filaments, and high-strength alloys.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Tungsten forms compounds such as tungsten carbide (WC) and tungsten trioxide (WO3).
💣
Physical Properties
Tungsten has a boiling point of 5555°C and a melting point of 3422°C.
🎯
Uses
Tungsten is used in electrical contacts, filaments, and high-strength alloys.
