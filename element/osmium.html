OSMIUM

Osmium is a dense and hard transition metal known for its extreme density.
It is used in electrical contacts, fountain pen tips, and as a catalyst in certain reactions.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Osmium forms compounds such as osmium tetroxide (OsO4) and osmium dioxide (OsO2).
💣
Physical Properties
Osmium has a boiling point of 5027°C and a melting point of 3033°C.
🎯
Uses
