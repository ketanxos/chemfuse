BARIUM

Barium is an alkaline earth metal used in various industrial applications and in medical imaging.
It is also used in fireworks and as a component in certain glasses.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Barium forms compounds such as barium sulfate (BaSO4) and barium chloride (BaCl2).
💣
Physical Properties
Barium has a boiling point of 1850°C and a melting point of 725°C.
🎯
Uses
Barium is used in medical imaging, in fireworks, and in certain glass formulations.
