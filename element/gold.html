GOLD

Gold is a precious metal known for its color and luster.
It is used in jewelry, electronics, and as a standard for monetary systems.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Gold forms compounds such as gold chloride (AuCl3) and gold oxide (Au2O3).
💣
Physical Properties
Gold has a boiling point of 2856°C and a melting point of 1064°C.
