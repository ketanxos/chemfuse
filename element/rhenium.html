RHENIUM

Rhenium is a transition metal used in high-temperature superalloys and catalysts.
It has one of the highest melting points and is used in various aerospace applications.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Rhenium forms compounds such as rhenium dichloride (ReCl2) and rhenium oxide (Re2O7).
💣
Physical Properties
Rhenium has a boiling point of 5596°C and a melting point of 3186°C.
🎯
Uses
Rhenium is used in high-temperature superalloys, catalysts, and aerospace components.
