SAMARIUM

Samarium is a lanthanide used in magnets and various high-performance materials.
It is also used in certain types of glass and in medical imaging.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Samarium forms compounds such as samarium oxide (Sm2O3) and samarium chloride (SmCl3).
💣
Physical Properties
Samarium has a boiling point of 1794°C and a melting point of 1072°C.
🎯
Uses
Samarium is used in magnets, glass, and medical imaging.
