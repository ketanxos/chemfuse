ZINC

Zinc is a blue-white metal used for galvanizing other metals.
It is also an essential trace element for living organisms.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Zinc reacts with acids and forms zinc salts. It is used to protect iron from corrosion.
💣
Physical Properties
Zinc has a boiling point of 907°C and a melting point of 419.5°C.
🎯
Uses
Zinc is used for galvanizing iron, in batteries, and as a dietary supplement.
