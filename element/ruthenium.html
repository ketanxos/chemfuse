RUTHENIUM

Ruthenium is a transition metal used in electronics and as a catalyst in various chemical reactions.
It is part of the platinum group of metals.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Ruthenium is highly resistant to corrosion and forms various oxidation states.
💣
Physical Properties
Ruthenium has a boiling point of 4150°C and a melting point of 2334°C.
🎯
Uses
Ruthenium is used in electronics, as a catalyst, and in jewelry.
