TITANIUM

Titanium is a strong, lightweight metal with a silvery color.
It is used in aerospace applications, medical implants, and high-performance alloys.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Titanium reacts with oxygen to form titanium dioxide. It also forms various alloys with aluminum and vanadium.
💣
Physical Properties
Titanium has a boiling point of 3287°C and a melting point of 1885°C.
🎯
Uses
Titanium is used in aerospace, medical implants, and high-strength alloys.
