LEAD

Lead is a heavy metal known for its density and softness.
It has been used in batteries, radiation shielding, and plumbing.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Lead forms compounds such as lead oxide (PbO) and lead chloride (PbCl2).
💣
Physical Properties
Lead has a boiling point of 1749°C and a melting point of 327.5°C.
🎯
Uses
Lead is used in batteries, radiation shielding, and plumbing.
