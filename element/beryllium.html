BERYLLIUM

Beryllium is a hard, grey-white metal that is relatively rare and highly toxic. It is used in aerospace and electronics.
It has a high melting point and is used as an alloying agent in various metals to increase their strength.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Beryllium reacts with acids but not with bases. It forms a protective oxide layer when exposed to air.
💣
Physical Properties
Beryllium has a boiling point of 2470°C and a melting point of 1287°C.
🎯
Uses
Beryllium is used in aerospace components, electronic connectors, and as an alloying agent in metals.
