ZIRCONIUM

Zirconium is a transition metal used in nuclear reactors and in various alloys.
It is a corrosion-resistant and high-strength material.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Zirconium reacts with air and forms a protective oxide layer, making it resistant to corrosion.
💣
Physical Properties
Zirconium has a boiling point of 1855°C and a melting point of 1852°C.
🎯
Uses
Zirconium is used in nuclear reactors, surgical instruments, and in high-temperature alloys.
