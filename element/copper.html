COPPER

Copper is a reddish-brown metal known for its electrical conductivity.
It is widely used in electrical wiring and plumbing.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Copper reacts with oxygen to form copper oxides. It also forms alloys with other metals, such as bronze and brass.
💣
Physical Properties
Copper has a boiling point of 2562°C and a melting point of 1085°C.
🎯
Uses
Copper is used in electrical wiring, plumbing, and in various alloys.
