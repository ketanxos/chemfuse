ARGON

Argon is a colorless, odorless noble gas found in the Earth's atmosphere.
It is used in lighting, welding, and as an inert atmosphere for various industrial processes.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Argon is chemically inert and does not react with other elements under standard conditions.
💣
Physical Properties
Argon has a boiling point of -185.8°C and a melting point of -189.34°C.
🎯
Uses
Argon is used in lighting, welding, and as an inert gas in various industrial applications.
