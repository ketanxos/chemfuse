TECHNETIUM

Technetium is a synthetic element used in medical imaging and as a tracer in research.
It is a radioactive element with no stable isotopes.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Technetium is chemically reactive and forms compounds like technetium dioxide.
💣
Physical Properties
Technetium has a boiling point of 4265°C and a melting point of 2157°C.
🎯
Uses
Technetium is used in medical imaging and as a radioactive tracer in research.
