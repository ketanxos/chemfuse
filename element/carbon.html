CARBON

Carbon is a non-metal and a fundamental building block of life. It is the fourth most abundant element in the universe.
It exists in various forms, including graphite, diamond, and carbon nanotubes.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Carbon forms a wide variety of compounds, including hydrocarbons and carbon dioxide. It is known for its catenation property.
💣
Physical Properties
Carbon has a boiling point of 4827°C and a melting point of 3550°C. It is a good conductor in its graphite form.
🎯
Uses
Carbon is used in steel production, as a component of organic compounds, and in water filtration systems.
