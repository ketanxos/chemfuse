TENNESINE

Tennessine is a synthetic chemical element with the symbol Ts and atomic number 117.
It is an extremely radioactive element that can only be created in a laboratory.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Tennessine is expected to have properties similar to other halogens, like chlorine and iodine. However, due to its short half-life, its chemical properties have not been well-studied.
💣
Physical Properties
Tennessine is a synthetic element, and its physical properties are not well-known due to its extremely short half-life. It is predicted to be a solid at room temperature.
🎯
Uses
Tennessine has no practical applications due to its radioactivity and short half-life. It is mainly used for scientific research purposes.
