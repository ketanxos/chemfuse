THALLIUM

Thallium is a soft, malleable metal with a silvery appearance.
It is used in electronics, glass manufacturing, and as a pesticide.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Thallium forms compounds such as thallium chloride (TlCl) and thallium sulfate (Tl2SO4).
💣
Physical Properties
Thallium has a boiling point of 1457°C and a melting point of 304°C.
🎯
Uses
Thallium is used in electronics, glass manufacturing, and as a pesticide.
