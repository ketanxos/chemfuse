EUROPIUM

Europium is a lanthanide used in phosphorescent materials and various high-performance applications.
It is also used in certain types of lasers and in medical imaging.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Europium forms compounds such as europium oxide (Eu2O3) and europium chloride (EuCl3).
💣
Physical Properties
Europium has a boiling point of 1527°C and a melting point of 822°C.
🎯
Uses
Europium is used in phosphorescent materials, lasers, and medical imaging.
