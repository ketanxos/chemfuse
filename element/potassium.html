POTASSIUM

Potassium is a highly reactive metal and is essential for various biological functions.
It is used in fertilizers and in the production of various chemicals.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Potassium reacts vigorously with water to form potassium hydroxide and hydrogen gas. It also forms compounds with many non-metals.
💣
Physical Properties
Potassium has a boiling point of 759°C and a melting point of 63.5°C.
🎯
Uses
Potassium is used in fertilizers, in the production of glass, and in various chemical syntheses.
