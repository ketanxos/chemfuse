CERIUM

Cerium is a lanthanide used in catalytic converters and in various high-performance materials.
It is also used in glass polishing and as a catalyst in chemical reactions.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Cerium forms compounds such as cerium oxide (CeO2) and cerium chloride (CeCl3).
💣
Physical Properties
Cerium has a boiling point of 3257°C and a melting point of 798°C.
🎯
Uses
Cerium is used in catalytic converters, glass polishing, and as a chemical catalyst.
