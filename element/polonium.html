POLONIUM

Polonium is a highly radioactive metalloid.
It is used in anti-static devices and as a neutron source.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Polonium forms compounds such as polonium dioxide (PoO2) and polonium hydride (PoH2).
💣
Physical Properties
Polonium has a boiling point of 962°C and a melting point of 254°C.
🎯
Uses
Polonium is used in anti-static devices and as a neutron source.
