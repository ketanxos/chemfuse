BROMINE

Bromine is a reddish-brown liquid at room temperature and is used in flame retardants.
It is also a halogen element.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Bromine is highly reactive and forms compounds with many elements. It is used in flame retardants and disinfectants.
💣
Physical Properties
Bromine has a boiling point of 58.8°C and a melting point of -7.2°C.
🎯
Uses
Bromine is used in flame retardants, water purification, and as a biocide.
