NITROGEN

Nitrogen is a colorless, odorless gas that makes up 78% of the Earth's atmosphere. It is used in various industrial applications.
It is inert under most conditions and forms compounds with most elements, except noble gases.
🧪
Chemical Properties
Nitrogen is relatively inert and reacts with oxygen to form nitrogen oxides under high temperatures.
💣
Physical Properties
Nitrogen has a boiling point of -195.79°C and a melting point of -210.00°C.
🎯
Uses
Nitrogen is used as an inert gas in industrial processes, in fertilizers, and in cryogenics.
